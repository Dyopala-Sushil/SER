Speech is the most natural way of expressing ourselves as humans. Speech Emotion Recognition (SER) is the process of extracting the emotional state of the speaker from his or her speech signal. About Model

Speech Emotion Recognition is based on the Convolution Neural Network (CNN) algorithm which uses different modules for the emotion recognition and the classifiers are used to differentiate emotions such as happiness, surprise, anger, neutral state, sadness etc. The dataset for the speech emotion recognition system is the speech samples and the characteristics are extracted from these speech samples using LIBROSA package. In fact, the emotional feature extraction is a main issue in the SER system. The important speech features contains emotion information such as energy, pitch, formant frequency, Linear Prediction Cepstral Coeeficients (LPCC), Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficient (MFCC) and Modulation Spectral Features (MSFs). Here, we extracted the MFCC features of any speech and by using it we specify the emotion of an individual by using CNN model.
